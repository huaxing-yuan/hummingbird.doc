Organization of services to test

In hummingbird test framework, services are organized in 3 levels:

  • Application : Represents an application to test. Or in the context of service simulation, represents an application to simulate.
  • Service Category: Functionality regroups some services into a category.
  • Service: The service to test itself.
Functionally organized

Other test software or similar solutions on the market usually just lists all services A to Z. Innovation made by Hummingbird is based on the experience of many testers, that adding Application and Service Category helps reducing the time needed to choose a service and gives testers a best way to connect the functionality of the service and it’s technical name.

In Hummingbird App, graphically, they are represented as above image. BackOffice Systems represents the services used in the Back Office application; Invoice Processing regroups 3 web service that is used in the context of Invoice. You can also found other service from another tabs.

When new services definition are imported, the service selector screen will show them in appropriate position.

Search the service

When you get familiar with the project and the services to test, you may use the Search function to find service faster, by typing for example convert in the search box, all matched services will be listed. Now you need a click on the service name to start edit test data (the Request)
